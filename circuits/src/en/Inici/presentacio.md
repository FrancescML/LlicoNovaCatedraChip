## Welcome to the digital circuits lessons from [jutge.org](https://jutge.org)

This educational resource is aimed at all pre-university students interested in learning the basics of digital circuit design.

The content is structured as the theoretical support for the course [*Introduction to Digital Circuit Design*](https://jutge.org/courses/JordiCortadella:IntroCircuits), which offers a series of exercises.

Access to the lessons is free of charge, but to complete the exercises you will need to register **for free** on the jutge.org platform.

[jutge.org](https://jutge.org) is an educational platform developed by the Polytechnic University of Catalonia (UPC). User registration has as its sole purpose the monitoring of progress in the didactic exercises, and it does not serve any commercial interest or collect personal data.

[jutge.org](https://jutge.org) offers other [lessons](https://lliçons.jutge.org/) that introduce basic programming knowledge in various languages, in addition to **this** course on digital circuits.
