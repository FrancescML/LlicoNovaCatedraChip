# TinyMicro

The section [Tiny Micro](https://jutge.org/courses/JordiCortadella:IntroCircuits/lists/JordiCortadella:TinyMicro) on Jutge is a collection of advanced exercises on the operation of a small computer. There are design exercises for an Arithmetic Logic Unit (**ALU**), programming the control unit, creating a datapath, and the design of a program counter.


## Exercises on Jutge.org: [Introduction to Digital Circuit Design](https://jutge.org/courses/JordiCortadella:IntroCircuits)

- [TinyMicro ALU](https://jutge.org/problems/X67117_en)
- [TinyMicro datapath](https://jutge.org/problems/X62350_en)
- [TinyMicro program counter](https://jutge.org/problems/X83229_en)
- [TinyMicro control unit](https://jutge.org/problems/X66986_en)

<small>Remember that to access the exercises and for the Judge to evaluate your solutions you must be enrolled in the [course](https://jutge.org/courses/JordiCortadella:IntroCircuits). You will find all the instructions [here](../Inici/instruccions.md).</small>
