This lesson contains a video showing how to use [Jutge.org](https://jutge.org) to solve a problem in Python.

The Judge is a platform for learning programming that contains a repository of problems. Judge users can submit their solutions to the problems and it issues a verdict on their correctness.

To use the Judge you need to register by creating a user account with an email address. Minors must also provide an email address of their parents or legal guardians, who will receive the registration information.

The Judge's problems are organized into lists within courses. To follow this Introduction to Programming course, we recommend signing up for the *Learning to program* course, as shown in the video. Your teachers can also invite you to their courses, which will contain their own problem lists.
